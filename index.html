My Work

MyNotebook

I designed this app in the context of a new client asking me to build a note-taking app à la “less, but better.” This client has not worked with a UI Designer before, so they haven’t prepared any of the information you normally receive regarding UX deliverables. My wireframes were expected to be shaped by functionalism, meaning they are clean, simple, and that I design with “less is more” in mind.

I.Do is an app that works as a personal wedding planner. After a detailed competitive analysis I have found the gap in the market and the missing pieces my app could fill in. DiVino

DiVino is a responsive web app which works as an online wine store. Its usability is defined by the wide variety of filters helping users select the information and customize their search. During this project I explored advanced UI skills, including how to prototype complex animations and interactivity into my app, and learned how to develop a cohesive brand.

MyTrip

MyTrip is a responsive location-based app focused on reviews and recommendations. During this project I explored the key steps in the UX design process, from user research, designing personas, and drawing user flows, to wireframe sketches and user testing. With a mobile-first approach to my responsive app, I adapted my designs for different breakpoints and completed the entire design process concluding with my project mockups.

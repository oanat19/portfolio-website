My Work

MyNotebook

I designed this app in the context of a new client asking me to build a note-taking app à la “less, but better.” This client has not worked with a UI Designer before, so they haven’t prepared any of the information you normally receive regarding UX deliverables. My wireframes were expected to be shaped by functionalism, meaning they are clean, simple, and that I design with “less is more” in mind.

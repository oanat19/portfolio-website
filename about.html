About

MyStory

Hi! My name is Oana. I am a professional photographer specializing in UX and UI Design. My background in photography, editing and layout design had helped me express my creativity in UI Design, making it my dream job. I am motivated by personal and professional development and I am excited about learning new things, becoming a challenge for me.


As an experienced sales person, focused on guest experience, I am able to easily discover and understand people’s needs. I am a team player, as I had been part of, as well as led one, and I understand the importance of collaboration in creating and delivering quality products. Years of traveling around the world and meeting very diverse people and cultures, had offered me a different perspective, and helped me become more flexible, in the same time giving me the ability to work in varied environments. In my spare time I love reading, dancing and learning new languages.
